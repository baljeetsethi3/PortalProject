ABOUT ACM-CSS

ACM-CSS is an officially recognised technical society of a college. It is headed by Akhil Singla,Btech,4th Year. Before it

is recognised as an official society,it struggled a lot to find space among technical socities.This society aims at fostering

the future of students by providing them real case scenarios.ACM-CSS Executive Body is the backbone of the society.They

have onus like conducting day-to-day workshops,holding code battles,organising techfest,collaborating with colleges. The

society has gained respectable status by achieving many laurels for the college.
